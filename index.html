01 / 05 — About

A studio grounded in place.

Bangalore, India · est. practice

At Tatva Studio, we believe that every space is a living dialogue between culture, climate, and community. Founded by Hema Perumalsamy, the studio is grounded in the idea that architecture must respond to its context — honoring tradition, embracing sustainability, and adapting with purpose.

From ecological materials to regionally rooted design principles, we craft spaces that are not only functional, but resonate with meaning, resilience, and belonging. With a shared commitment to contextual design and environmental responsibility, we bring together a diverse team of architects, designers, and strategists who believe that spaces should evolve from the land, culture, and communities they serve.

"Guided by curiosity and care, we collaborate closely with clients to craft bespoke solutions — infusing every project with sensitivity, innovation, and a deep respect for place." — Tatva Studio, design philosophy

Whether it's a humble dwelling or a large-scale intervention, our work seeks harmony between the built and the natural, the old and the emerging.

04 / 05 — Selected work

Five projects, one thread of intent.

Residential · Interior · Institutional
01 Anil Dev Residence
Residential +
A bespoke private residence where neo-classical elegance meets contemporary comfort. The exterior showcases refined classical proportions, while the interiors embrace a sleek, modern palette. Precision-manufactured units were sourced from China, and sustainability was woven into the design through the use of Wienerberger clay blocks, ensuring thermal efficiency and reduced environmental impact.
Bangalore, India
02 HC Office
Interior +
A strategic interior facelift that redefined the spatial experience of a complex CA office. The design balances functionality with finesse, delivering robust, contemporary solutions that optimize space while exuding understated luxury. Every detail — from layout reconfiguration to material palette — was curated to reflect professionalism, efficiency, and elevated aesthetics.
H.C. Khincha & Co., Bangalore
03 Vedalaya School
Institutional +
A purpose-driven campus nestled in the rural fabric of Madipadu, designed to foster learning in harmony with nature. Rooted in vernacular sensibilities and ecological responsibility, Vedalaya embraces passive cooling strategies, local materials, and modular spatial planning — reflecting the Gurukulam philosophy of a space as grounded and nurturing as the pedagogy it supports.
Madipadu, India
04 Sringeri Residence
Residential +
Set against the lush, mist-laden backdrop of Sringeri, this residence is a thoughtful response to both climate and context. Faced with the challenges of high humidity and monsoon-driven terrain, the design strikes a balance between traditional wisdom and modern sensibilities — a fusion of vernacular aesthetics and contemporary detailing that preserves heritage while embracing durability.
Sringeri, Karnataka
05 Vadiraj Residence
Residential +
On a modest site, this home brings to life a family's dream of owning a cozy yet luxurious retreat — crafted with care, and within budget. Thoughtfully designed contemporary interiors maximize both space and comfort, making every corner feel expansive and elegant. A home where affordability meets aspiration, without compromising on detail or warmth.
Bangalore, India

05 / 05 — The humans behind the harmony

A studio of two disciplines, one philosophy.

Hema
Founder & Principal Architect

Hema is the driving force behind Tatva Studio — an architect who blends intuition with insight. A strategist and systems thinker, she operates at the intersection of design, ecology, and culture. Her expertise spans sustainable architecture, contextual design, and the revitalization of traditional systems through a contemporary lens. She builds with purpose — crafting spaces that are intuitive, resilient, and deeply rooted in place.

Suritha
Design Manager

With over 25 years in architecture, Suritha brings a wealth of experience, clarity, and grounded wisdom to Tatva Studio. Her portfolio spans projects including Biocon Labs, Brigade Millennium, AstraZeneca, Reliance, Aster Cauvery Medical Center, and the National Institute of Design, Bangalore. At Tatva, she collaborates closely across all phases — from concept to execution — helping shape projects with strategic insight and deep contextual understanding.
